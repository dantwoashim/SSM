# Targeting Guide

## Best first customers

- Series A-C B2B SaaS
- 10-100 employees
- Selling upmarket
- Publicly mention SSO, SAML, SCIM, directory sync, or enterprise security
- Likely ACV above $20k

## Signals to prioritize

- Enterprise pricing page
- SSO/SCIM docs or changelog
- Security questionnaire or trust center
- Jobs mentioning enterprise auth, IAM, or solutions engineering
- Customer-facing launch of role mapping or provisioning
