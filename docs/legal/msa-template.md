# Master Services Agreement Template Outline

## Core sections

- Parties and effective date
- Services definition for identity assurance engagements
- Fees, invoicing, and payment terms
- Customer responsibilities for environment access and test credentials
- Confidentiality
- Data handling and security controls
- Intellectual property and reusable methodologies
- Warranty disclaimers and limitation of liability
- Term, termination, and survival

## Service-specific clauses

- Engagements validate scoped environments only
- Reports are based on evidence captured during the defined review window
- Findings do not constitute legal or compliance attestation
- Customer remains responsible for final production configuration and access control decisions
