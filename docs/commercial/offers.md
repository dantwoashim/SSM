# Commercial Offers

## Core promise

We identify and document what will break in an enterprise identity rollout before the buyer's IAM team finds it.

## Deal Rescue

- Scope: one environment, one target customer, one primary IdP, one report, one retest.
- SLA: 72 hours from complete intake and access confirmation.
- Price: $4k-$7k for the first five pilots.
- Deliverables:
  - Executive summary
  - Scenario matrix
  - Findings with severity
  - Buyer-safe remediation notes
  - Retest result

## Launch Sprint

- Scope: multi-cycle validation plus remediation support.
- Price: $12k-$25k.
- Add-ons:
  - Customer IT review call
  - Role mapping deep dive
  - Release-gating rehearsal

## Annual Assurance

- Scope: quarterly regression checks and priority support for high-value enterprise deals.
- Price: $30k-$80k annually.
- Target buyer: CTO, enterprise product lead, head of engineering.
